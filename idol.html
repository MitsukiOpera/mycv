Son Tung M-TP

Famous singer, songwriter, and music producer from Vietnam. Son Tung M-TP is known for his hit songs and unique musical style.

Biography

Son Tung M-TP, whose real name is Nguyen Thanh Tung, was born on July 5, 1994 in Thai Binh, Vietnam. He started his music career at a very young age and quickly became one of the most famous artists in Vietnam with a series of successful songs.

Outstanding hits

During his career, Son Tung M-TP has released many hit songs such as "Lac Troi", "Chay Ngay Di", "Hay Trao Cho Anh", and many others. His hits are not only successful in Vietnam but also loved in many other countries.
